About Me

Hi! My name is Michal, and I’m a photographer with a passion for capturing authentic emotions and creating timeless memories. Over the years, I’ve also developed a strong interest in web design, internet technologies, and how to combine creativity with the principles of IT and modern online communication.

My professional background includes more than 15 years of office-based work in Poland. I spent six years at PKO Bank Polski, where I was responsible for customer service and building long-term client relationships. After that, I worked for seven years at PZU, Poland’s leading insurance company, handling data entry and providing administrative support to insurance agents. I also spent two years at Proengineering, a company specializing in industrial machinery design and construction, where I managed production orders requiring precision and excellent organizational skills.

After relocating to the UK, I gained experience in fast-paced warehouse environments, where flexibility, efficiency, and resilience under pressure were essential. This diverse career path has equipped me with strong analytical, organizational, and problem-solving skills—competencies that are equally valuable in IT and digital projects.

At the same time, I’ve been developing my own online projects. I run social media accounts under the brand “suffering but with style” , where I discuss topics like mental health and everyday struggles with a lighthearted and self-ironic touch. My goal is to build a community based on honesty, humor, and support.

My ambition is to combine my professional experience with my passion for the Internet and new technologies—from web design and social media management to broader IT-related tasks. I’m ready to bring dedication, creativity, and practical skills to any project where technology meets people.

Thank you for visiting my website, and feel free to reach out!

Best regards,
